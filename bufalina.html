La Bufalina

Serving Size: Small, Medium, Large

Price: R87.00, R118.00, R156.00



Description:

Renowned for its rich flavor and tender texture, buffalo mozzarella gives this traditional pizza variety its distinctive, one-of-a-kind flavor. Pizza bufalina is typically finished off with a drizzle of olive oil and a handful of fresh basil leaves before serving.
